    • CookieYes GDPR Cookie Banner

    Company: Reviewbit

    Company Website: www.reviewbit.app

    Role: Backend Developer

    Technology Used: Laravel, MySQL, RestFull API, Git, BitBucket, ReactJs

    Duration: October 2023 - January 2024

    Project Overview: CookieYes is a cloud-based platform for GDPR and CCPA compliance that assists in managing ePrivacy and other international privacy laws. It enables businesses to scan their websites to detect and categorize cookies automatically. CookieYes offers an audit table that helps to compile a list of all the cookies used by the website and their detailed description.
    * Single-click automatic scanning and categorization of cookies.
    * Fully customizable cookie banner that matches the aesthetics of your website.
    * Automatically block third-party scripts until the user gives consent.
    * Record and maintain a log of the cookie consent you receive from the visitors.
    * Free privacy and cookie policy generator.
