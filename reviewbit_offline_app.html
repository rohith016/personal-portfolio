    • Reviewbit Reviews via WhatsApp

    Company: Reviewbit

    Company Website: www.reviewbit.app

    Role: Lead Backend Developer

    Technology Used: Laravel, MongoDB, MySQL, RestFull API, POS API, Git, Github, ReactJs, Whatsapp API, Nginx, GCP, Meta API, Stripe API

    Duration: January 2024 - May 2024

    Project Overview: A review app to send automated, personalized review requests. Which offers a notably higher conversion rate than email requests. The ease of sending photos & videos via WhatsApp will prompt customers to respond readily. Create an emotional bond with customers via an intuitive chat experience. Send separate follow-up messages for star ratings, and text & photo reviews and motivate customers to leave reviews. Display reviews in attractive widgets to spur hesitant buyers to make quick decisions.
    * Personalized message templates that can be edited to match your brand voice.
    * Gather and Showcase Photo and Video Reviews
    * Multiple customizable display options to showcase user-generated content.
    * Send review requests in 11 languages from WhatsApp-Business API-verified number.
    * Send discounts to happy customers and offer prompt service to the unhappy ones
